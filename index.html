About Startup Ireland

Startup Ireland is a grassroots network connecting the rapidly evolving communities which support the Irish startup ecosystem. Startup Ireland was born following discussions between those active partners in the Irish startup ecosystem on the best model for such a community led organisation. Inspired by the Startup America Partnership we resolved to shine a light on indigenous entrepreneurial activity in Ireland.

Impactful, dynamic networks are powered by grassroots structures and community-minded ideas and people - Startup America

Influenced by the thinking of Brad Feld and the Kauffman Foundation , Startup Ireland believes that successful startup ecosystems are created from the ground up. What startup entrepreneurs need most is the mentorship and fellowship of other entrepreneurs who can help them avoid missteps and point them toward customers, funders and talent - Kauffman

Our Goals

Ireland has many of the natural advantages needed to be a leading international startup location. Our over-riding aim is to help our capital achieve a top 20 position in the international ranking of startup ecosystems compiled by the Startup Genome.

Initially the Startup Ireland partnership will focus on fostering connections between the startup communities. We are also collecting data on Irish startups to demonstrate to the international community of investors and entrepreneurs the strength and depth of the Irish startup ecosystem.
